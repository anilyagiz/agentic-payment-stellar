# Demo Day Presentation Outline

## 1. Problem

- Agentic workflows need payment rails that are fast, programmable, and auditable.
- Most teams do not want to manage raw signing, fee logic, or reconciliation themselves.

## 2. Solution

- StellarAgent provides API-key based registration, deterministic fee splitting, fee sponsorship, and transaction indexing.
- The dashboard shows live metrics, earnings, and operational status.

## 3. Monetization

- Free onboarding for pilots
- Pro plan for production teams
- Scale plan for high-volume operators
- Enterprise plan for custom onboarding and support

## 4. Proof

- Metrics dashboard: `/dashboard`
